How to Contact Isaac?

If you want to contact me, you can email me at isaacmaddox05@gmail.com. Alternatively, you can go to this page and leave a comment.

Information

My "office hours" are from 3:00pm to 6:00pm every afternoon on weekdays. On weekends, I will be able to respond almost any time between 9:00am and 6:00pm. Please do not expect a response outside of these hours that I have set. Thank you for your patience.

What to send Isaac?

I will read any emails that I recieve, but I may not respond to all of them. I especially like emails with examples of what you have created using Simplistic Design. I will also definitely pay attention to bug reports.

Emails with Examples

If you are sending me some code to an example of Simplistic Design in action, please make the subject "SD Example", or something similar.

Emails with Bug Reports

I will pay special attention to emails that have to do with bug reports, as I would like to keep Simplistic Design as bug-free as possible. For an email concerning bugs, please send with the title "SD Bug Report", or something very similar. I will make sure to respond and get the bug fixed as fast as I can. In the body, please include a description of the bug, as well as a screenshot, if applicable. In the release notes of the release in which the bug is fixed, I will credit the first person who I saw send me the email. I will not dig through my inbox to find the first person to report this. Sorry for any inconvenience this may cause.
